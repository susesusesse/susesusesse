ASCENDRA

Brand identity

This project involves the creation of a visual identity, a website, and an advertising campaign for a jewelry brand called Ascendra.

Self-edited and self-published.
It is an up-and-coming brand based in Barcelona that specializes in designing and customizing handcrafted jewelry with a modern and alternative style.

Physical materials: Business cards, stickers, and necessary stationery. Also posters to be placed in public spaces in Barcelona and other nearby cities.

Digital materials: Website (mobile and desktop), reel-format audiovisual ads, and photo montages for social media and the website.
